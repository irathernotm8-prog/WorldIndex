# Centennial

*After Valkorath -- present day*

## The Story Setting

A hundred years and more removed from Aridel and the age of knights, Centennial finds Aluccard -- immortal legend turned tech CEO -- confronted with a new kind of war: someone, or something, is killing immortal beings. With a threat to his own existence at stake, he sets out to take the fight to an unknown enemy, uncovering old allies, older enemies, and a few faces that reach all the way back to the age of gods.

Lucan, an immortal prince, is the first of them to die. His murder sends Aluccard down a path that reconnects him with Leon, his trained mortal apprentice; Drexl Voss, brought back from death through magic and machinery; Arthur, still walking the world as a spirit made physical through will alone; and a widening circle of the strange and ancient trying to survive in the 21st century -- gods gone corporate, kings who walked away from their crowns, and at least one reaper who'd rather run a jazz bar than do his job.

*This is a story outline and premise rather than a chapter-by-chapter narrative -- the full prose telling hasn't been written yet.*
