# Aridel

*After Valkorath*

## The Story Setting

Set in a period more closely resembling the middle ages, we follow a land -- Aridel -- in disarray, pillaging and plunder abundant. The age of knights has come and gone, and everyone acts in self-interest. Longer still past are the days of magic and wonder; the age of man has forgotten what other powers once populated the world.

We enter on a would-be king of what would one day be called England -- though it wasn't called that yet -- Edwin, as he attempts to unify the people and bring a sense of dignity back to the land of Aridel.

To that end, he enlists the help of one Drexl, a mercenary who fights like a demon, so they say, and his reluctant partner, Aluccard. Together with the king's trusted and only knight, Arthur -- yes, that one, of legend -- they set out to unify the land and bring it under Edwin, taking down each of the would-be despots controlling their own portion of it.

After a long adventure, Edwin gets his wish -- but not without many a sacrifice, and not without losing the vision he once started with. It's the path England, and the world, remains on to this very day.

*This is a story outline and premise rather than a chapter-by-chapter narrative -- the full prose telling hasn't been written yet.*
